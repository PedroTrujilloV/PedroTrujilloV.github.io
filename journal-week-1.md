#**Perseverance vs fortune**

## *"Perseverance overcomes when fortune is not enough"* 

This famous Colombian saying summarizes the reality implicit in the power of effort an individual who has not been rewarded by nature with exceptional genes or gifts, and / or those who were not lucky enough to be born in a place with resources necessary for optimal development. In her book *The Growth Mindsets*, **Dr. Carol Dweck**, presents in her research that the key to instruct a human is to motivate and reward the effort to learn. Complimenting and rewarding natural gifts is a bad practice because it does not contribute to learning and instead generates conformity.


###What group am I?

I think that I am in the second group (not naturally endowed), since throughout my academic life I have not been a person of high scores. But it is something that doesn’t discourage me, since I am now someone who strives to learn something new every day. Throughout my life my struggle has not only been for me, but also for those who love and those who really want and try to please, God, my mother and today my smart beautiful and persevering wife. And now I thank God for it.
